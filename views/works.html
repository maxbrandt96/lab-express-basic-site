Kevin Parker's Discography

Innerspeaker

Released in 2010, Innerspeaker is Kevin Parker's debut album as Tame Impala. The album features a psychedelic and psychedelic rock sound, and is known for its layered and lush production.

Lonerism

Released in 2012, Lonerism is Tame Impala's second studio album. The album features a mix of psychedelic rock and pop music, and was highly acclaimed for its dreamy and introspective sound.

Currents

Released in 2015, Currents is Tame Impala's third studio album. The album marked a departure from the psychedelic rock sound of the band's previous albums, and instead incorporates elements of funk, R&B, and electronica.

The Slow Rush

Released in 2020, The Slow Rush is Tame Impala's fourth studio album. The album features a psychedelic pop and funk sound, and explores themes of growth, change, and the passage of time.
